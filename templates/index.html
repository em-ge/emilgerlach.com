about me

In 2023, I started learning to code as a teenager using Swift Playgrounds. By now, I've tried a bit of everything (Python, Godot, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Flask, Turtle, Pygame, Strudel, Swift, Go). I'm still learning a bit of everything.
I only code as a hobby for fun in my free time, so I'm definitely not professional.
Beyond coding, I'm fascinated by history.
I enjoy listening to music and playing my Tele (a guitar) as well.
I've been playing chess since childhood. Now I play on lichess.org and chess.com low-elo chess. Occasionally, I also play AoE2. Most of the time, I call myself 'em-ge' online.
